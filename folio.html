about folio.

I am an avid journal keeper. I have been since middle school. Therefore everything that has happened to me or I have thought has been written down somewhere for me to infrequently glance back through. Last year I scanned them all and uploaded them to an LLM, and then posted the results here; for all to see and for people to one shot me in the future.

Turns out the only things you can buy right now are like fake excerpts from etsy and no other great options. Therefore this prompted me to want to make my own diary selling and buying marketplace.

for sellers: set your price. publish under your name or a pen name. choose one copy or many. we take 15%.

for buyers: scroll through real people's real years. read an excerpt. buy the one that speaks to you.

the one rule: it has to be real. no AI-generated journals. we check.
