marketing philosophy

Growth isn't about ads—it's about understanding human psychology, cultural moments, and the mechanics of virality. The best marketing feels like it's not marketing at all. It's about creating things people genuinely want to share, building loops that sustain themselves, and knowing exactly when to push and when to let organic momentum take over.
