05 Why Blueberry

Built for B2C, from the ground up.

There's no shortage of social tools. Blueberry is the only one that starts and ends with revenue — built specifically for consumer brands who sell through social.

Revenue-first, not engagement-first

Every feature is built around one question: did this make money? Likes and impressions are table stakes. Blueberry shows you attributed revenue from every post and every conversation.

Your brand voice, not generic AI

Our AI is trained on your tone, product catalog, and customer history. Every response sounds like you — not a chatbot. Consistent across millions of conversations without adding headcount.

One platform for the full funnel

Social listening, DM automation, influencer tracking, revenue attribution — all in one workspace. No more switching between tools and losing context on what converted.

Setup in under 30 minutes

Connect your channels, upload your brand guidelines, and go live in one click. No engineering required. Most customers see their first attributed conversion within 48 hours.
