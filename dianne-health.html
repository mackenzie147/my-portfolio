Carotid Artery Narrowing
Being monitored
The carotid arteries carry blood to your brain. One side is significantly narrowed, while the other has only minor narrowing. Your doctors are closely watching this.
Call 911 immediately if you suddenly experience
  • Weakness or numbness on one side of your face, arm, or leg
  • Trouble speaking or understanding words
  • Vision problems in one or both eyes
  • A very sudden, severe headache
  • Dizziness or sudden loss of balance
These may be signs of a stroke — every minute counts.
