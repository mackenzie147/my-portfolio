who we are

Common Good TO is a volunteer-run network for Toronto builders who want their skills to matter. We don't take a cut. We don't sell your data. We curate jobs, aggregate bounties, and surface open problems — then get out of the way.

The platform runs on agents so nothing goes stale. Built by one person who got tired of seeing great builders in Toronto not know where the real work was.
