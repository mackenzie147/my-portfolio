Design notes
Customers first
Lead with brand logos above the fold
Warriors, Mellow Sleep, and Impact Dog Crates before any product description. Visitors decide trust in 0.05 seconds — big brand names convert better than copy.
Source: Nielsen Norman Group, "First Impressions"
Blueberry physics 🫐
Matter.js real physics — berries spill as you scroll
Each white section gets 12 custom-drawn SVG blueberries (radial gradients, shine highlights, crown divot, stem). They tumble from a cluster at the top and settle at the bottom. Triggers once per section when it enters the viewport. The content sits above the physics layer — readable at all times.
Brand ticker bar
Customer names replace emoji in the header bar
Golden State Warriors, Mellow Sleep, Alo Yoga, etc. scrolling in the blue bar = instant social proof on every page, before you've read a word.
Receipt placement
Loss aversion converts 2× better than gain framing
Showing what someone is losing outperforms showing what they'd gain. Kahneman's Prospect Theory.
Source: Kahneman & Tversky, Prospect Theory 1979
Turing test
Interactive demos convert 3× better than static
Making visitors do something is dramatically more engaging than reading about the product.
Source: Navattic "State of Interactive Demos" 2024
ROI calculator
Calculators are the #1 converting B2B tool
ROI calculators generate 3× more leads — give visitors a personal number to take back to their team.
Source: Demand Gen Report 2023
